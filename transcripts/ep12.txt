Summary:
In this special episode, AJ Wilcox sits down with Ting Ba, Head of Product Marketing for LinkedIn Pages, to unpack the latest features you 
need
 to know about—especially if you run LinkedIn Ads.
Ting shares the newest updates, including employee notifications, team recognition tools, and the new Page completion meter—all designed to boost visibility, trust, and engagement for your brand. They also dive into how LinkedIn Pages serve both recruitment and brand-building goals, why video and LinkedIn Live are exploding in reach, and how marketers can strategically activate employees as brand advocates.
Whether you’re a page admin, B2B advertiser, or just trying to grow your company’s presence, this episode gives you actionable insights straight from the source. Don’t miss it!
