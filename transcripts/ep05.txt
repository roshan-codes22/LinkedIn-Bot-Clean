An Interview with Tom Kim, Senior Product Marketing Manager at LinkedIn
AJ Wilcox
March 3, 2020
•
Welcome to the LinkedIn Ads Show On this episode, I will be interviewing Tom Kim at LinkedIn. Contact us at Podcast@B2Linked.com with ideas for what you’d like AJ to cover.

Summary:
This episode explores how to 
navigate LinkedIn Campaign Manager more efficiently
, featuring insights from the team behind its user experience. You’ll learn how to choose the 
right campaign objective
, why it’s essential to pick a single KPI, and how the platform now aligns 
bidding, optimization, and reporting
 to your marketing goals. New features like 
objective-based clicks
 ensure you’re only charged for meaningful actions—such as landing page visits or form submissions—helping you focus on what truly drives results.
The conversation also covers 
auto vs. CPC bidding
, targeting best practices, and why 
ad rotation shouldn’t be used for A/B testing
. There’s advice for new advertisers, tips for navigating the new interface, and reassurance that the team is actively working on key feature requests like campaign deletion and duplication.
